### Challenges & Lessons

* **Challenges:**
  * RAG concepts are complex; vector embeddings and similarity search require deeper mathematical understanding.
  * Team had different visions for the project; required multiple discussions to align.

* **Solutions:**
  * Watched YouTube tutorials on embeddings and vector databases to build intuition.
  * Created a shared Miro board to visualize everyone's ideas before voting.

* **Lessons Learned:**
  * RAG is powerful but requires proper data preparation (chunking strategy matters).
  * Early team alignment saves time later; resolving conflicts early prevents bigger issues.
